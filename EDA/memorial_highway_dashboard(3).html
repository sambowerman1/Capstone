Historical Eras of Honorees

Most honorees were born in the early-to-mid 20th century. The peak birth decade is the 1920s. Deaths cluster in the 1960s–2010s, suggesting highways named for recently deceased figures.

Highway Categories

Classifying all 5,335 names by theme: the majority honor individual people, but a substantial 874 (16%) are military/veteran memorials, 550 (10%) honor law enforcement and first responders, and 37 are explicitly MLK Jr. highways. Political figure naming accounts for 3%.

Honoree Lifespan Analysis

Among the 214 honorees with both birth and death years, the mean lifespan is 70.3 years. Female honorees lived significantly longer on average (81.1 years) than male honorees (68.7 years). Honorees born in the 1900–1919 window — the WWII generation — lived the longest at an average of 80.2 years.
